2018–19 figure skating season

The 2018–19 figure skating season began on July 1, 2018, and ends on June 30, 2019. During this season, elite skaters will compete on the ISU Championship level at the 2019 European, Four Continents, World Junior, and World Championships. They will also compete in elite events such as the Grand Prix series and Junior Grand Prix series, culminating in the Grand Prix Final, and the ISU Challenger Series.


Medalists so far

At this point in the season, as far as the Grand Prix Series goes, Skate America, Skate Canada, the Grand Prix of Helsinki and NHK Trophy are over.
The following skaters took home the medals:

Skate America (19-21 of October)

Men:

Gold: Nathan Chen of the USA
Silver: Michal Březina of Czechia
Bronze: Sergei Bornov of Russia

Ladies:

Gold: Satoko Miyahara of Japan
Silver: Kaori Sakamoto of Japan
Bronze: Sofia Samodurova of Russia

Skate Canada (26-28 of October)
